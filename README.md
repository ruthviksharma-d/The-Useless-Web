## What Is This

You click a button. You get teleported to a random corner of the internet. It might be a horse with infinitely long legs. It might be someone slapping a stranger with an eel. It might be a website that just says "no" dramatically for 30 seconds.

That's it. That's the product.

No sign-up. No onboarding flow. No "we noticed you haven't completed your profile." Just pure, uncut internet chaos served cold.

## Philosophy

The modern internet is optimized. Personalized. Monetized. Every pixel is A/B tested. Every scroll is tracked. Every recommendation is a calculation.

This is the opposite of that.

Useless Universe is a reminder that people built things on the internet just because they could. Because it was funny. Because it was 2 AM and they had an idea. The web used to feel like discovery ‚ÄĒ like you could wander into something completely unexpected.

We're trying to bottle that.
